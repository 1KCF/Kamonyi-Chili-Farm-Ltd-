ABOUT

Kamonyi Chili Farm (KFC) is a youth owned supplier of fresh and dried chili peppers. Research shows that small number of youth are involved in agriculture sector as there is a myth of it being for uneducated people. However, Rwanda is enforcing the market-oriented Agriculture and encouraging many young people to create jobs, especially in the agriculture sector. Therefore, my purpose for this business is to make it a number one Chili farm that makes high-quality grade A and B chili source in the region. Hence, our aim is to make it a million dollar worth by 2024
